#01

Start with Mission, Not Org Chart

  • Align team to clear enterprise mission tied to business value, not technology
  • Define winning in 12–24 months: impact on operations, cost, growth, decision intelligence
  • Rally around purpose & measurable outcomes, not activity
Signal: Strategic leader, not operational manager
#02

Hire for "Builders + Scalers"

  • Builders — innovators, 0→1 creators
  • Scalers — production, reliability, MLOps, platform thinkers
  • Translators — bridge business and technology
  • Avoid teams of only researchers or only engineers
  • High performance comes from complementary capability mix
Signal: Built real AI orgs, not theoretical ones
