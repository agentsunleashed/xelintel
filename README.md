# XELINTEL

**VP of AI Interview Intelligence Reference**

A fast, searchable cheat sheet for VP of AI interviews at Fortune 10 companies. Instantly pull up frameworks, key phrases, and structured thinking for any topic.

## Features

- **Instant Search** — `Ctrl+K` to search across all topics
- **Collapsible Sections** — Click any header to expand/collapse
- **Sidebar Navigation** — Jump to any topic instantly
- **Keyboard Shortcuts** — `Ctrl+K` search, `Esc` clear
- **Mobile Responsive** — Works on any device
- **Print Friendly** — Clean print layout

## Topics Covered

- AI Strategy & Vision
- Technical Architecture & Infrastructure
- Business Impact & ROI
- Team Building & Organization
- Leadership & Executive Presence
- Interview Frameworks & Questions
- Industry Trends & Emerging Tech
- Technical Deep Dives
- AI Metrics & KPIs

## Usage

Open `index.html` in any browser, or visit the GitHub Pages deployment.
